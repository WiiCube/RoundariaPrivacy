Privacy Policy for Roundaria

Roundaria does not directly collect personal information from players. However, the game may use third-party services to provide advertising, analytics, and in-app purchases. These services may automatically collect certain anonymous information required for their functionality.

Third-party services used by the game may include services provided by Google and Unity, such as Google Play services, Google Play Billing, AdMob, and Unity Ads. These services may collect limited information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, crash logs, and basic interaction data in order to provide advertisements, process purchases, and maintain service stability.

The information collected by these third-party services is handled according to their own privacy policies. Advertisements shown in the game may be personalized or non-personalized depending on user consent and regional privacy regulations.

Roundaria itself does not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties for independent marketing purposes.
