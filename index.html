Privacy Policy for Roundaria

《Roundaria》does not currently collect any personal information from players beyond what is necessary for gameplay. The game may include third-party services for advertising and in-app purchases, such as AdMob, Unity Ads, or Google Play Billing, which may collect minimal anonymous data for advertising, analytics, and payment functionality. These data may include device identifiers, crash logs, and in-app purchase information. None of this data can directly identify you personally.

We do not share your personal information with third parties for unrelated purposes. Any data collected by third-party services is used solely to provide advertising, analytics, and payment functionality and is transmitted securely over encrypted connections.

For users under 13 years old, we comply with Google Play’s Families Policy and do not collect personal information from children beyond what is necessary for gameplay or required by the third-party services mentioned above. Collected data may be automatically deleted or anonymized within 90 days.
